Data Collection & Analysis

Delivering marketing performance requires precise measurement. If handled properly, data can transform every aspect of a business. But the volume, variety, and complexity of information means that there is more to it than crunching a few numbers. Data may be the fuel, but analytics is the engine that turns it into energy. Data has always been difficult to manage. That's why we use analytics to solve complex business problems. From procurement and supply chain to marketing and business growth, Sygnific's advanced analytics team has the experience, skills and tools to deliver tangible solutions. Our team understands that analytics is about competence and curiosity. With this important combination in mind, we hire and nurture the right talent to help you answer your most pressing questions.

Our analytics services also include dashboard design and hands-on training for your team to ensure you get the most insight from your data. Whether your preferred data visualization tool is Google DataStudio, Tableau, or Domo, we can configure your dashboards to provide the answers you need to grow your business. In addition, our team of web analysts and tracking experts offer solutions for conversion rate optimization (CR0) and user experience (UX) enhancements, so you can make the most of your data activation.
